About the Le Mans Virtual Series

The first four races in the Le Mans Virtual Series were held between September and December 2021, with the 24 Hours of Le Mans Virtual taking place as the climax to the premier endurance esports championship at the start of 2022.

The finale will be a live, televised event on 15/16 January with 50% of the entire US$250,000 prize fund available for the 24 Hours of Le Mans Virtual standalone race.
